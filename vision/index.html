Our Vision

Building reliable electric mobility for India — with a global mindset.

Saffire EV by Koyo Gears India Pvt. Ltd. is focused on creating practical, reliable and business-ready electric mobility platforms. We begin with electric three-wheelers for passenger and cargo movement, while building towards a future ecosystem of connected EVs, two-wheelers and compact commercial mobility solutions.

Our Mission

To build electric vehicles that solve real mobility and business needs — combining strength, affordability, reliability and efficient electric performance for Indian roads.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Saffire Smart Ride?

Saffire Smart Ride is a premium D+3 passenger electric three-wheeler designed by Koyo Gears India Pvt. Ltd. for urban mobility and driver efficiency.

How many passengers is it designed for?

It features a practical D+3 layout, accommodating the driver and up to 3 passengers.

What is the battery capacity?

It is equipped with a 14.61 kWh (63V 232Ah) battery.

What is the claimed range?

The claimed range is 200 km per full charge. Please note that range and performance may vary based on load, route, driving conditions, and temperature.

Is it suitable for daily passenger routes?

Yes, it is built with a heavy-duty reinforced chassis and an efficient electric drivetrain specifically intended for reliable, daily urban passenger transport.

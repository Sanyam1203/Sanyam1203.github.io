Saffire Swiss Cargo

Electric Cargo Loader Built to Carry. Built to Last.

Saffire Swiss Cargo is the loader-focused electric three-wheeler from Saffire EV by Koyo Gears India Pvt. Ltd., engineered for India’s demanding cargo and last-mile logistics needs. Built around a reinforced structure, long wheelbase and efficient electric drivetrain, Swiss Cargo is designed to support business owners, fleet operators and delivery users who need dependable payload movement with lower operating cost.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Saffire Swiss Cargo?

Saffire Swiss Cargo is a robust electric cargo loader designed for commercial payload movement and last-mile logistics.

What is the payload capacity of Saffire Swiss Cargo?

It has a strong payload capacity of 600 kg.

What is the battery capacity?

It is equipped with a 14.61 kWh (63V 232Ah) battery.

What is the claimed range?

The claimed range is 200 km per full charge. Range and performance may vary based on load, route, driving conditions, and temperature.

Is it suitable for last-mile delivery?

Absolutely, it is specifically intended for local delivery, market supply movement, and commercial cargo operations.

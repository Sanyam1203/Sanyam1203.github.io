Premium Electric Passenger Auto

The shift towards sustainable public transport has made the electric passenger auto a vital component of urban mobility. As cities prioritize clean air and reduced noise pollution, Saffire EV is dedicated to providing electric passenger three-wheeler solutions that benefit both the drivers and the commuters.

Designed for Comfort and Earnings

For auto drivers, the vehicle is their livelihood. Our electric passenger auto is built to maximize daily operational efficiency. By drastically reducing the running costs compared to traditional CNG or diesel rickshaws, drivers can take home a larger portion of their daily earnings.

Simultaneously, the passenger experience is elevated. The electric drivetrain ensures a smooth, vibration-free, and quiet ride, making the Saffire EV electric passenger three-wheeler an attractive choice for daily commuters navigating busy city streets.

Empowering Local Mobility

Ergonomic Design

Suitable for long shifts, the seating and cabin layout are engineered to reduce driver fatigue while providing ample space for passengers.

Reliable Powertrain

Created for Indian business mobility, the robust battery and motor combination provides the necessary range and torque to handle varying terrains and passenger loads.

A Smart Investment

Upgrading to an L5 EV passenger auto represents a forward-thinking investment. For entrepreneurs exploring an EV dealership in India, the demand for reliable passenger mobility solutions presents a significant market opportunity.

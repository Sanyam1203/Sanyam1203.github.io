Electric Cargo Loader for Business Logistics

The modern logistics and delivery sector in India is rapidly evolving. For businesses looking to optimize their last-mile delivery operations, finding a reliable and efficient commercial vehicle is paramount. The electric cargo loader is emerging as the optimal solution for these challenges, and Saffire EV by Koyo Gears India Pvt. Ltd. is focused on providing vehicles designed specifically for this demand.

Built for Indian Business Mobility

An electric cargo three-wheeler must endure rigorous daily use, navigate narrow urban corridors, and handle substantial payloads without compromising on range. Our electric cargo loader models are intended for exactly these commercial environments.
